Making our home your home

Quality aged care since 1992.

Matamata Country Lodge is located in six acres of award winning grounds and gardens and is close to the town centre of Matamata, situated in the heart of the Waikato, the ideal choice for retirement living.

We offer a total care concept with a wide range of accommodation available from chalets through to rest home and hospital level care. We offer the highest standard of staff and management to take care of all your health and security needs.
